About Quantum Quill

Welcome to Quantum Quill, a digital service platform where creativity meets innovation. We specialize in offering top-notch services like web development, graphic designing, video editing, and more. At Quantum Quill, we don’t just create; we bring visions to life. Whether you're an individual or a business, we provide you with the tools to make your digital presence stand out.

Founded by two visionary individuals, Ritesh Poudel and Aadarsh Khatri, Quantum Quill was built with the goal of pushing boundaries and setting new standards in the digital services industry.

Ritesh Poudel

CEO
At just 17 years old, Ritesh is the creative force behind Quantum Quill. With a passion for technology that started in 2020, Ritesh faced many challenges but never gave up on his dream. A product of South Point Boarding High School, his creativity and drive have shaped Quantum Quill into what it is today. He’s determined to ensure that Quantum Quill’s journey to success is limitless.

Aadarsh Khatri

Group Learning Coordinator
Aadarsh, currently studying in 10th grade, plays a crucial role at Quantum Quill as the coordinator of group learning at the National Academy. Like Ritesh, Aadarsh’s dedication to his craft and passion for innovation has contributed to the company’s vision. His leadership and commitment to success mirror Ritesh’s, as both strive for excellence in everything they do.

Join us on our journey to bring the best digital solutions to the world.
